FAQ

PRODUCTS

  • What kinda products do you sell?

We sell unisex T-shirts (Half and Full Sleeves), Hoodies.

  • How do I reach you for Customized Bulk Orders?

To get a customized T-shirt for your gang, send us your design, requirements, and contact details to info@barcelona.in or WhatsApp us on 612345678. The minimum order quantity for bulk order is 25.

  • Do we have any specific handling instructions?

We send it with love, so please do handle with care. For T-shirts, just do not iron on the print!! Simple!!

PAYMENT:

  • How can I confirm, if my order has reached Clothes Stores?

If your order has bee successfully processed, you will first receive an email and SMS from Instamojo regarding payment confirmation and followed by email and SMS from Clothes Stores regarding your order confirmation. Email should be listed under Social, Updates or Promotions mailbox if not on your primary email box.

  • I have received payment confirmation email but not order confirmation email from Clothes Stores?

Kindly wait for couple of minutes to see you receive the order confirmation email and SMS. Else, please reach out to support@barcelona.in for further queries.

  • Do we have a Cash on Delivery option for our purchase?

No! We currently do not have COD option in our website.

  • Do you charge for shipping and what is the minimum order value for availing free shipping option?

No! The shipping charges are absolutely on us and there are no minimum order value criteria for the same.

ORDER CANCELLATION

  • How to cancel my order?

Cancellation of a full or partial order is possible when intimated via email, to support@barcelona.in within 6 hours of placing the order, and only if the order has not been processed, also shipped orders cannot be canceled.

ADDITIONAL COMMENTS SECTION

  • What are the benefits of additional comments section in checkout page ?

Add a special instruction to Meesakar team, this can either be accommodated or rejected and varies case to case. We do not confirm and process unless we acknowledge back about your request.

  • I am ordering for someone, where should I provide the contact number of the receiving person ?

Please mention the contact number in "additional comments" section during checkout process. If no number has been mentioned, we will consider the contact number used for ordering the product.
